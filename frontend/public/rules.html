Platform Rules & Guidelines

Please read and follow these important rules to ensure a safe and fair experience for all users.

Account Rules

  • One Account Per User

    Users are strictly limited to one account per person. Multiple accounts or sharing accounts is prohibited.

    Violation will result in account termination and forfeiture of earnings.

  • Account Security

    Users must maintain secure passwords and never share their login credentials. Enable two-factor authentication for added security.

  • Accurate Information

    All account information must be accurate and up-to-date, including contact details and payment information.

Financial Rules

  • Withdrawal Limits

    Minimum withdrawal amount is $5 USDT. Maximum withdrawal per transaction is $50 USDT. 24-hour cooldown period between withdrawals.

  • Payment Methods

    All transactions must be conducted through Binance USDT. No other payment methods are currently accepted.

  • Processing Time

    Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Recharge confirmations may take up to 24 hours to reflect in your account.

Referral Program Rules

  • Referral Requirements

    Only active users can participate in the referral program. Referred users must be genuine and unique individuals.

  • Commission Structure

    Earn commission from your referrals' activities. Commission rates vary based on your membership level and referral tier.

  • Prohibited Practices

    Self-referrals, fake accounts, or any form of referral manipulation will result in immediate account termination.

Content & Behavior Rules

  • Respectful Conduct

    Users must maintain respectful behavior towards other members. Harassment, abuse, or discrimination will not be tolerated.

  • Content Guidelines

    All user-generated content must comply with our community guidelines. No illegal, harmful, or inappropriate content.

  • Privacy Respect

    Respect the privacy of other users. Do not share personal information or attempt to bypass platform security measures.

Enforcement & Penalties

  • Rule Violations

    Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination based on severity.

  • Appeals Process

    Users can appeal enforcement actions through our support system within 7 days of the action.

  • Updates to Rules

    E-Tube reserves the right to modify these rules at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes.
